The highest peak in all the realms

Finally able to make the journey to Jotunheim with the help of Mimir's Bitforst eyes, Kratos and Atreus reach a Giant temple - one that has their entire journey carved into the walls. The Giants has seen what would come to pass, as well as what had come before.

They discover that this was all left for them by Faye, who was a Giant hersalf - a secret she had kept from her husband and son. Atreus finallu uncovers the full truth of his heritage, that he is not only part god and part mortal, he is part Ginat as well.

Atreus had two names, one was given to him by his father and the other, his mother. The name Faye had bestowed upon him was Loki.

Together Kratos and Atreus scatterr the ashes on the peak of Jotunheim, finally fulfilling their quest.
